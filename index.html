Retirement Tools

A small collection of exploratory calculators I built for my own retirement modelling and scenario testing, shared in case they’re useful to others. Where relevant, these tools are designed for Australians and reflect Australian superannuation settings and rules, with assumptions clearly visible and adjustable.

These tools help you explore assumptions and see how different drawdown choices behave under uncertainty. They are not advice tools, and results may be inaccurate, incomplete, or wrong.
